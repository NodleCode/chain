Module polkadot_primitives::v0::fisherman[−][src]

Expand description

App-specific crypto used for reporting equivocation/misbehavior in BABE, GRANDPA and Parachains, described in the white paper as the fisherman role. Any rewards for misbehavior reporting will be paid out to this account.
